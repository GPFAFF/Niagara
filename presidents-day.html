Presidents Day Tournament

February 17th - 19th 2024

This 3 day event will be held at the newly renovated Hyde Park Ice Pavilion in Niagara Falls, NY and continues to attract teams from around the U.S. and Canada. The rink has undergone a complete overhaul (see photo section). If you haven’t been there lately, you may not recognize it. Both rink have new locker rooms, ices surfaces, scoreboard, benches and stands, plus the Storeroom bar and restaurant are a great addition. Last year’s tournament was sold out again with 52 teams so apply early.

With 52 teams in attendance, last year’s event delivered plenty of memories and excitement for all who attended. six of our Championship games were decided by one goal or less, with the average goal differential for the entire tournament was 2.25.

This year’s tournament will feature AA, A, B, AE, Select, MD and MOHL level teams in the Mite, Squirts, Pee Wee, Bantam and Midget Divisions. Teams from Florida, Texas, Massachusetts, Ohio, Pennsylvania, NY, NJ, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Michigan, Illinois, Rhode Island, Washington D.C., Maryland, Maine, Texas, and Ontario, Canada have been invited. Forty to Forty Eight Teams will be accepted.

Each Team will have a minimum of four games and the maximum number of games is dependent on the number of teams in your division.

Teams should be ready to play as early as 7:00 AM Saturday February 17th with Final Championships concluding by 5:00 PM February 19th.

For more information about the rules, screening process for teams, and tournament features, please click on the Information button below. If you are ready to apply, click on the Register now button or give Mike a call for more info. 716-316-1526
