Festival of Lights Tournament

November 24th - November 26th 2023

This flagship tournament of NST is in its 28th season and is as competitive as ever. This year's event will be held at the completely renovated Hyde Park Ice Pavilion (see photo section) in Niagara Falls, NY. Over the last 4 years the rink has went through a complete overhaul. Both rink have new locker rooms, ices surfaces, scoreboard, benches and stands, plus the Stoneroom bar and restaurant is in full swing. Last year’s tournament had 48 teams that competed, with 5 of the championship games being decided by one goal! The average goal differential per game for the entire tournament was 2.25!.

This year’s tournament will feature AA, A, B, AE, MD, Select, MOHL level teams in the Mite, Squirts, Pee Wee, Bantam and Midget Divisions. Teams from Florida, Texas, Massachusetts, California, Ohio, Pennsylvania, NY, NJ, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Michigan, Illinois, Rhode Island, Washington DC, Maryland, Maine, and Ontario, Canada have been invited.

Each teams will play 4-6 games with games times starting at 7:00AM on Friday November 24th with final games concluding on Sunday November 26th by 5:00PM.

Each Team will have a minimum of four games and the maximum number of games is dependent on the number of teams in your division.
