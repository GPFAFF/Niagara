Dear Coach/Manager,

Congratulations! Your team has been given conditional acceptance to participate in the Tournaments.

Final acceptance will be confirmed upon receipt of a verified USA / CHA Hockey roster along with the enclosed forms. Please fill out a copy of the following forms: team roster, hotel selection, and rooming list. Please submit an official verified USA Hockey roster or equivalent as soon as you can along with 13 copies of game sheet labels listing your roster. Please return the hotel selection and rooming list form as soon as possible. You will be able to print a copy of these forms on your computer.

Remember all teams must stay at our host hotels in order to be accepted into our tournament. Hotel reservations will be made by our reservation committee. You cannot make your own reservations at our host hotels. A copy of your rooming list must be sent or faxed to us. You will have the best selection of hotels if you respond early. Please advise us of your preference in making reservations. Final confirmation will not be given until all forms are received. Do not delay the return of these forms! It may affect your acceptance. Remember, first come -first served.

The game schedule will be emailed to you as soon as scheduling is completed. If a division or subdivision does not fill, we will contact you immediately and your money will be refunded.

We look forward to seeing you and your team. Get ready for a great experience.

Sincerely yours,

Mike Carella
