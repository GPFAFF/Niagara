Niagara Falls Blizzard Challenge Tournament

January 13th - 15th 2018 (Saturday-Monday)

This year's event will be held at the completely renovated Hyde Park Ice Pavilion (see photo section) in Niagara Falls, NY and usually sell out by mid November. Over the last few seasons, the rink has been completely overhauled. Both rink have new locker rooms, ices surfaces, scoreboard, benches and stands, plus the Stoneroom bar and restaurant is a great addition to the rinks. With 54 teams in attendance, last year’s event delivered plenty of memories and excitement for all who attended. Five of our Championship games were decided by one goal or less, with the average goal differential for the entire tournament was 2.15

This year’s tournament will feature AA, A, B, AE, Select and MOHL level teams in the Mite, Squirts, Pee Wee, Bantam and Midget Divisions. Teams from Florida, Texas, Massachusetts, Ohio, Pennsylvania , NY, NJ, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Michigan, Illinois, Rhode Island , Washington DC, Maryland, Maine, and Ontario, Canada have attended in the past. A maximum of 54 Teams will be accepted.

Each Team will be guaranteed a minimum of three games with a maximum of 5 games. The tournament schedule will be emailed to accepted teams 3-4 weeks in advance of the event.

Teams should be ready to play as early as 7:00 AM January 13th with Final Championships concluding by 7:00 PM January 15th.
