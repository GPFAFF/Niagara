Power City Bruins Early Bird

August 26th - 28th 2022

NST is teaming up with our local youth organization to kick off this season with a new tournament. We are back for another exciting year, and, we want to get thing going early this season. Tournaments early in the season are usually geared for Midgets and Bantams, but we are going to also try and run Peewee and Squirt divisions. This tournament will be used as a fundraiser for the Power City Bruins Youth Hockey Association.

This 3 day event will be held at the completely renovated Hyde Park Ice Pavilion in Niagara Falls, NY and sells out earlier every year. If you haven’t been to the rink lately, you may not recognize it. Both rink have new locker rooms, ices surfaces, scoreboard, benches and stands, plus the Stoneroom bar and restaurant is up and running. With a sold out field of 50 teams in attendance, last year’s event delivered plenty of memories and excitement for all who attended. With the midget seasons at full swing, this tournament tends to have all ages of Midgets. Last year was the first where we had 1/2 ice Mites and it worked out great. As always we will have all divisions Mite to Midgets, so Give Mike a call for more info. 716-316-1526

This year’s tournament will feature AA, A, B, AE, Select and MOHL teams in the Mite, Squirts, Peewee, Bantam and Midget Divisions.. Teams from Florida, Texas, Massachusetts, Ohio, Pennsylvania , NY, NJ, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Michigan, Illinois, Rhode Island Washington DC, Maryland, Maine, and Ontario, Canada have been attendees in previous years. Forty to Forty Eight Teams will be accepted.

Each Team will have a minimum of four games and the maximum number of games is dependent on the number of teams in your division.

Teams should be ready to play as early as 7:00 AM August 26th with Final Championships concluding by 5:00 PM August 28th

For more information about the rules, screening process for teams, and tournament features, please click on the Information button below. If you are ready to apply, click on the Register now button or give Mike a call for more info. 716-316-1526
