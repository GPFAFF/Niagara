August 20th - 22nd 2021

NST is teaming up with our local youth organization to kick off this season with a new tournament. With COVID-19 stopping last yearsâ€™ tournaments, we want to get thing going early this season. This tournament will be used as a fundraiser for the Power City Bruins Youth Hockey Association.
