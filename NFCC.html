Niagara Sports Tournaments

Niagara Falls Championship Cup

March 10th - 12th , 2018 (Friday-Sunday)
"Who will take home the Cup?"

This year's event will be held at the completely renovated Hyde Park Ice Pavilion (see photo section) in Niagara Falls, NY. Over the last 5 seasons, the rink has went through a complete overhaul. Both rink have new locker rooms, ices surfaces, scoreboard, benches and stands, plus the Stoneroom bar and restaurant is up and running. With 64 teams in attendance, last year’s event was the largest March tournament we ever had and also our most competitive with 7 of the 13 championship games being decided by 1 goal

This year’s tournament will feature AA, A, B, AE, Select, MD, and MOHL level teams in the Mite, Squirts, Pee Wee, Bantam and Midget Divisions. Teams from Florida, Texas, Massachusetts, Ohio, Pennsylvania, NY, NJ, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Michigan, Illinois, Rhode Island, Washington D.C., Maryland, Maine, and Ontario, Canada have been in attendence in the past.

Each Team will be guaranteed a minimum of three games with a maximum of five games which is dependent on the number of teams in your division. The tournament schedule will be emailed to accepted teams 3-4 weeks in advance of the event.

Teams should be ready to play as early as 7:00 AM Friday, March 10th with Final Championships concluding by 5:00 PM Sunday March 12th.
